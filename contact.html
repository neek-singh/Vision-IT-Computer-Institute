Common Questions

Aksar Pooche Jaane Wale Sawaal

Admission ke liye kya documents chahiye?
Admission ke liye aapko sirf ek valid ID proof (Aadhaar Card, School ID) aur 2 passport size photos chahiye. Koi minimum qualification required nahi hai — sabke liye open hai.
Fees kitni hai aur installment kaise milegi?
Courses ₹2,000 se shuru hote hain. 3+ months ke courses mein easy installment facility available hai. Pehle month ki fees admission ke samay jama karni hoti hai, baaki EMI mein.
Certificate kitne time mein milega?
Course complete hone ke 15-30 din baad certificate issue hota hai. Certificate government recognised hai aur sarkari/private job applications dono mein valid hai.
Kya online classes available hain?
Abhi hamare saare courses offline hain — center par aake sikhna practical skills ke liye sabse better hai. Online notes aur study material WhatsApp group pe milta hai.
Inquiry ke baad kitne time mein call aata hai?
Online form submit karne ke 24 ghante ke andar hum personally call karte hain. WhatsApp pe message karne par generally 1-2 ghante mein reply milta hai. Urgent ho to seedha call karein: +91 81031 70595.
