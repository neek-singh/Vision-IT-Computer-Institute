Vision IT kab establish hua?
Vision IT Computer Institute 2019 mein Pratappur, Surajpur (CG) mein shuru hua. Shuruat se hi hamar focus practical skills aur affordable education raha hai.
Kya certificates sarkari naukri mein valid hain?
Haan! Hamare sabhi certificates government recognised hain — sarkari naukri, private jobs aur higher education mein valid. Lifetime validity, kabhi expire nahi hota.

Koi minimum qualification nahi! 5th class se graduate tak sab apply kar sakte hain. Basic course ke liye zero computer knowledge bhi theek hai.

Courses ₹2,000 se start hote hain. Cash, UPI ya EMI — sabhi options available hain.
