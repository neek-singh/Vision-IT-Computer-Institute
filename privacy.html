Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

At Vision IT Computer Institute ("we," "our," or "us"), we are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our website or apply for admission, we may collect:

  • Name, email address, and mobile number
  • Course interest and batch preference
  • Date of birth and educational qualification
  • Messages you send through the contact form
  • Account information when you register

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process admission inquiries and contact you about courses
  • To send important updates about your course or batch
  • To verify and issue certificates
  • To improve our website and services
  • To send newsletters (you can unsubscribe anytime)

3. Data Security

We use Firebase by Google for secure data storage. Your data is encrypted and stored securely. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

4. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your experience (such as remembering your dark/light mode preference). You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work properly.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Google Firebase for authentication and data storage, and Google Maps for location services. These services have their own privacy policies.

6. Your Rights

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications
  • Request data portability

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide our services. Certificate and result data is kept permanently for verification purposes.

8. Contact Us

For privacy-related queries, email us at: visionitpratappur@gmail.com or visit us at Kadampara Chowk, Pratappur, Surajpur, CG 497223.
