FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Aapke common sawaalon ke jawab yahan hain

Courses ki fees kitni hai?
Hamari courses fees ₹2,000 se start hoti hai. Basic Computer ₹2,500, Advanced ₹4,500, Programming ₹5,500. Installment bhi available hai. Discount courses ke liye check karein.
Certificate government recognized hai?
Haan, hamare certificates government recognized hain aur industry-accepted hain. Job applications mein, government exams mein aur higher studies mein valid hain.
Batch timing kya hai?
Teen batch available hain — Morning 9:00 AM, Afternoon 2:00 PM, aur Evening 5:00 PM. Aap apni convenience ke hisab se choose kar sakte hain.
Placement support milega?
Haan! Course complete karne ke baad hum placement assistance dete hain — resume building, interview prep, aur job referrals. Hamara 70%+ placement record hai.
Pehle se computer knowledge zaroori hai?
Bilkul nahi! Hamare Basic Computer Course specifically un students ke liye hai jinhe zero knowledge hai. Hum bilkul scratch se shuru karte hain.
Online classes available hain?
Abhi hamare saare courses offline / center par hain. Online courses launching soon hain — WhatsApp group join karein updates ke liye!
Fees installment mein de sakte hain?
Haan! 3-6 month courses ke liye easy installment facility available hai. Details ke liye directly contact karein ya admission form submit karein.

Last updated: January 2025

When you use our website or apply for admission, we may collect:

  • Name, email address, and mobile number
  • Course interest and batch preference
  • Messages you send through the contact form
  • Account information when you register

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process admission inquiries and contact you about courses
  • To send important updates about your course or batch
  • To improve our website and services
  • To send newsletters (you can unsubscribe anytime)

3. Data Security

We use Firebase by Google for secure data storage. Your data is encrypted and stored securely. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Our website may use cookies to enhance your experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work properly.

We use Google Firebase for authentication and data storage, and Google Maps for location services. These services have their own privacy policies.

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications
