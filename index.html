FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Aapke common sawaalon ke jawab yahan hain

Courses ki fees kitni hai?
Hamari courses fees ₹2,000 se start hoti hai. Basic Computer ₹2,500, Tally GST ₹2,000, Web Design ₹3,500, Programming ₹5,500. Installment bhi available hai.
Certificate government recognized hai?
Haan, hamare certificates government recognized hain aur industry-accepted hain. Job applications, government exams aur higher studies mein valid hain.
Batch timing kya hai?
Teen batch available hain — Morning 9:00 AM, Afternoon 2:00 PM, aur Evening 5:00 PM. Apni convenience ke hisab se choose karein.
Placement support milega?
Haan! Course complete karne ke baad hum placement assistance dete hain — resume building, interview prep, aur job referrals. Hamara 70%+ placement record hai.
Pehle se computer knowledge zaroori hai?
Bilkul nahi! Hamare Basic Computer Course specifically un students ke liye hai jinhe zero knowledge hai. Bilkul scratch se shuru karte hain.
Fees installment mein de sakte hain?
Haan! 3-6 month courses ke liye easy installment facility available hai. Details ke liye directly contact karein ya admission form submit karein.
